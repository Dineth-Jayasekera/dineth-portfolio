About Me

A brief insight into my professional journey and passion for technology.

I am a highly motivated Software Engineering professional with deep expertise in cloud technologies, cybersecurity, and modern software development. With a strong academic foundation spanning from a BSc. in Software Engineering to a Ph.D. in Cyber Security Administration, I combine theoretical depth with hands-on engineering experience.

My career is driven by a passion for building secure, resilient systems that stand up to real-world threats. I specialise in AWS cloud architecture, containerisation with Docker, and implementing robust CI/CD pipelines that accelerate delivery while maintaining the highest security standards.

In the realm of cybersecurity, I apply OWASP principles and information security governance frameworks to ensure applications and infrastructure are protected against evolving threats. I believe security is not an afterthought — it is the foundation of great software engineering.

When I'm not architecting cloud solutions or hardening systems, I enjoy exploring emerging technologies, contributing to the developer community, and mentoring the next generation of software engineers.
